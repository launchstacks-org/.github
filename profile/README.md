## What we do

We are a small, senior engineering team that designs, builds, and supports modern SaaS and web platforms — primarily for founders in **healthcare**, **fintech**, **commerce**, and **trust & safety**.

Three things sit at the centre of every engagement:

| | |
| :-- | :-- |
| **AI-native** | Modern AI capabilities designed into the product from the first discovery call — streaming experiences, evaluation harnesses, cost guardrails, and fallback paths treated as production engineering. |
| **4 to 6 weeks to launch** | A working product on your own domain in four to six weeks. Weekly demos with real data, before you have burned a quarter on planning. |

## What we bring

- **AI integration done thoughtfully** — LLMs treated as production dependencies. Streaming UIs, eval suites, cached responses, fallback paths, cost limits.
- **Working build in 4 to 6 weeks** — From the first discovery call to a live, useful product on your own domain. Weekly demos on staging with real data, so you always have a working product to share.
- **Quality your team can audit** — Typed APIs, structured logs, traces, and tests where they actually catch bugs. The patterns reviewers, auditors, and future acquirers expect.
- **Code your future hire can grow into** — Documented, conventionally structured, with runbooks. A new engineer becomes productive in days, not quarters.
- **A partnership beyond launch** — Most engagements continue with monthly retainer support, ongoing roadmap work, and on-call response when something breaks.
- **Direct access to engineers** — The person on your kickoff call writes the code. No account managers in the middle.
